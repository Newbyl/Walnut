What happens when I enter a command in Walnut?

Introduction

Walnut is a free software program written by Hamoon Mousavi to solve problems and answer questions, posed in first-order logic, about automatic and related sequences. It can handle a wide variety of problems. There are some recent additions to Walnut, written by Aseem Baranwal.

Summary

Here are the three key steps of the command in Walnut:

  1. The command is received by the software and redirected to the corresponding method (Prover.java).
  2. Processing of the predicate (Computer.java).
  3. Walnut translates the formula into a predicate (Predicate.java).
  4. How act() works on every tokens.
